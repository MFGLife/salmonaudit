Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Salmon.

Salmon uses a sophisticated framework of archetypes and ideological populations to understand your worldview. Through initial assessments and ongoing interactions, it adapts its recommendations, information filtering, and communication style to align with your values while maintaining factual accuracy.

The community policing system allows users to submit geofenced reports with video evidence of incidents. These reports are verified through multiple methods including timestamp verification, geolocation confirmation, and cross-referencing with other users' reports. The system builds a trust score for reporters and creates heatmaps of community concerns that can be addressed collectively.

While Salmon can handle many routine legal tasks autonomously, complex cases may still benefit from human legal expertise. Salmon is designed to augment and democratize legal services, not necessarily replace lawyers entirely. It can prepare documents, file motions, and provide guidance, but for high-stakes litigation or nuanced legal strategy, consulting with a qualified attorney may still be advisable.

The 1GB size is carefully optimized to include all core functionalities while remaining lightweight enough to run efficiently on most devices. While larger models might have more general knowledge, Salmon's specialized training in legal and ideological domains means it performs exceptionally well within its designed scope. The compact size also enhances privacy by allowing local processing of sensitive data.
